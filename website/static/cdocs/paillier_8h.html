These are the decryption steps modulo n. The computations are carried out modulo p and q and combined using the CRT.

  1. \( ctl = ct^l \pmod{n^2} - 1 \)
  2. \( ctln = ctl / n \)
  3. \( pt = ctln * m \pmod{n} \)

Pick large prime numbers of the same size \( p \) and \( q \)

  1. \( n = pq \)
  2. \( g = n + 1 \)
  3. \( l = (p-1)(q-1) \)
  4. \( m = l^{-1} \pmod{n} \)
